What to include in your inquiry

  • Site Conditions: climate zone, dust load, humidity, panel orientation, and annual soiling data if available
  • Coating Interest: Quartz, Titan, or comparative evaluation
  • Performance Goals: cleaning reduction, yield recovery, and maintenance objectives
  • Maintenance Context: current O&M structure, access constraints, and cleaning methodology
  • Pilot Scope: array size, preferred timeline, and monitoring availability

Inquiry process

SolarEX follows a structured sequence designed to move from initial contact to a defined technical or commercial next step with minimal friction.

1

Initial Contact

Submit the inquiry by email. SolarEX reviews the request and acknowledges receipt.

2

Technical Fit

Site profile, project stage, and objectives are assessed against the coating performance envelope.

3

Pilot Evaluation

For qualified projects, SolarEX can co-design a structured pilot with defined parameters and monitoring logic.

4

Follow-Up and Scale

Pilot outcomes inform commercial framework, larger deployment, or partnership discussions.
